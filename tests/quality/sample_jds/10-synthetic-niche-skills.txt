Senior Growth Marketer
New York, NY (Hybrid 3 days/week)

About us

We're a Series-B health-tech company building the future of mental-health care. Our
mission is to make therapy accessible to every American.

Role overview

We're looking for a Senior Growth Marketer to own paid acquisition across Meta,
TikTok, Reddit, and Google. You'll partner with our brand team on creative strategy,
build out the analytics stack on Mixpanel and Segment, and own LTV/CAC modeling.

What you'll do

- Own paid-acquisition strategy across Meta Ads Manager, TikTok Ads Manager, Reddit
  Ads, and Google Ads.
- Build attribution models in Heap, Amplitude, or Mixpanel that account for view-through
  conversions and assisted attribution.
- Partner with the data team on Snowplow event instrumentation.
- Manage a quarterly budget of $2-3M across channels with weekly pacing reviews.
- Build LTV/CAC and payback period models in Looker Studio.
- Conduct creative testing in collaboration with our in-house Figma design team.
- Run quarterly business reviews with leadership.

Requirements

- 5+ years of paid-acquisition experience in DTC or B2C SaaS.
- Hands-on with Meta, TikTok, Reddit, and Google Ads platforms.
- Mixpanel, Heap, or Amplitude expertise; familiarity with Segment CDP, Snowplow, or
  RudderStack.
- Strong SQL skills.
- Comfort with attribution-modeling concepts (multi-touch, view-through, post-IDFA).

Nice-to-have

- Experience with Klaviyo, Iterable, or Customer.io for lifecycle marketing.
- Past work in regulated industries (health, finance).
- Familiarity with creative-testing platforms like Motion or AdLibrary.io.

Total compensation

Base $135K-$165K plus equity in early-stage startup.
