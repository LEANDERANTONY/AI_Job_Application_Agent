Senior Backend Engineer (Payments Platform)

München, Deutschland · Berlin, Deutschland · Remote (EU only)

About the company

We're a Series-B fintech building cross-border payment rails for European SMEs. Backed
by Index Ventures, Accel, and Earlybird. 180 people across Munich, Berlin, and Paris.

The role

You'll join the Payments Platform team — 12 engineers building the core ledger,
settlement, and reconciliation systems that move €4B/year across our customers. You'll
report to the Tech Lead Manager and work closely with our Compliance and Treasury teams.

What we're looking for

We expect at least 5 years of professional experience building production payment or
financial-services systems.

You should have:
- Strong proficiency in Java, Kotlin, or Go.
- Experience with PostgreSQL or similar relational databases at scale.
- Hands-on with event-driven architectures (Kafka, Pulsar, or RabbitMQ).
- Comfort with regulated environments (PCI DSS, PSD2, AML/KYC).
- Fluent business English; German is a plus but not required.

You might also have:
- Prior work at a fintech, neobank, or payment-services provider.
- Experience with double-entry accounting concepts.
- Track record of integrating with banking partners (SWIFT, SEPA, ACH).

What we offer

- Base salary: €95,000 - €130,000 depending on experience.
- Equity in a fast-growing Series-B company.
- 30 days of paid leave per year.
- Annual learning budget of €2,500.
- Visa sponsorship for relocation candidates.

We are an equal-opportunity employer and welcome candidates from all backgrounds.
