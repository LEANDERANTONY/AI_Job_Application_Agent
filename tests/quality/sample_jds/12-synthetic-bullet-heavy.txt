Site Reliability Engineer (Senior)
Remote (US/Canada/UK only) · Full time

What you'll do

• Operate the production observability stack (Prometheus, Grafana, Loki) for a fleet of
  ~3,500 Kubernetes pods across 7 regions.
• Lead incident response as the on-call commander; participate in 1 week per 6-week
  rotation.
• Design and own SLOs and error budgets across the platform.
• Build and maintain Terraform modules used by every product team.
• Partner with security on vulnerability triage and remediation across base images.
• Mentor junior SREs through their first on-call shifts.

What we're looking for

• 5+ years of SRE / DevOps / production-engineering experience.
• Deep experience with Kubernetes, including control-plane internals.
• Hands-on with Prometheus, Grafana, Loki, Tempo, or similar observability stacks.
• Proficiency in Terraform, Ansible, or Pulumi.
• Strong scripting skills in Python or Go.
• Comfort with on-call rotations and incident response.

Bonus points

• Past experience with AWS, GCP, or multi-cloud setups.
• Background in security, compliance (SOC 2, ISO 27001), or governance.
• Open-source contributions to CNCF projects.

Comp & benefits

We offer competitive base salary ($170K-$210K USD), equity, a $4K annual learning
budget, and remote stipend. Full medical/dental/vision coverage in supported regions.
