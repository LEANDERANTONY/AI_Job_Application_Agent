Senior Data Scientist (Marketing Analytics)

Remote · United States · Full-time · Mid-Senior

About the role

We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join the Marketing Analytics team. You'll
partner with brand and growth teams to design experiments, build attribution models, and
turn customer-behavior data into action.

Responsibilities

- Design and analyze A/B tests for paid acquisition, email, and onsite experiments.
- Build and maintain marketing-mix models that inform budget allocation across channels.
- Develop customer-segmentation models from first-party behavioral data.
- Collaborate with engineering on telemetry instrumentation and data quality.
- Translate analytical findings into clear narratives for non-technical stakeholders.

Required qualifications

- 5+ years of experience in analytics, data science, or applied statistics.
- Advanced SQL skills and proficiency in Python (pandas, scikit-learn).
- Hands-on experience with experimentation platforms (Statsig, Optimizely, or similar).
- Familiarity with attribution modeling and causal-inference techniques.
- Master's or PhD in Statistics, Economics, or related quantitative field.

Preferred qualifications

- Experience with Looker, Tableau, or other BI tools.
- Background in consumer or e-commerce companies.
- Comfort with cloud data warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift).

Compensation

The base salary range for this role is $145,000-$195,000 depending on level and location.
