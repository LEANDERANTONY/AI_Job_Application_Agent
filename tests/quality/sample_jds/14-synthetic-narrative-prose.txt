Director of Product, Platform

Hi! We're a small, fast-growing infrastructure startup. We're looking for our first
Director of Product to own the platform roadmap as we scale from 12 to 35 engineers
over the next two years.

This isn't a traditional PM job. We don't have separate "PM" and "engineering" tracks
— our PMs are deeply technical, ship features themselves where appropriate, and own
their products end-to-end. If you're drawn to high-ambiguity environments and want to
build the product function from the ground up, you'll thrive here.

About you

You've been in product at an infrastructure or developer-tools company for at least
seven years, and you've shipped products that engineers love. You've worked closely with
deeply technical founders and you've translated big technical ideas into roadmaps that
ship.

You should be comfortable with the messiness of an early-stage startup. We don't have
a polished playbook. We have customers, real revenue, and a strong technical foundation,
but a lot of how product gets built here is still being figured out.

What you'll do

You'll own the platform roadmap end-to-end. You'll write PRDs, run customer interviews,
prioritize backlogs, and partner with engineering leads on technical execution. You'll
also be the most senior product hire, so you'll set the bar for how we hire and develop
the rest of the product team as we grow.

We particularly value candidates with experience at companies like Stripe, Vercel,
Datadog, or Snowflake. Familiarity with Kubernetes, Terraform, or developer-experience
products is a plus.

About the company

We're based in San Francisco. We have an office in SOMA but most of our team works
hybrid. We offer competitive comp, equity, and full benefits. The salary range for
this role is $260K-$325K base plus equity.
