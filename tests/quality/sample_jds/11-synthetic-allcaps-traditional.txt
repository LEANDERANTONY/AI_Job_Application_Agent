SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT ENGINEER III, AWS LAMBDA

LOCATION: SEATTLE, WA (US) OR VANCOUVER, BC (CANADA)

JOB SUMMARY

AWS Lambda is the world's most widely used serverless compute platform. We are
looking for an SDE III to join the data-plane team, where you'll work on the systems
that execute customer code in millions of containers per day.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

* Design, implement, and operate distributed systems written in Rust, Go, or C++.
* Lead the technical execution of multi-quarter initiatives spanning 4-6 engineers.
* Conduct code reviews and mentor junior engineers on best practices.
* Own production incidents end-to-end including post-mortems and remediation.
* Partner with product managers and architects to define technical roadmaps.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS

* 6+ years of professional software-engineering experience.
* Strong programming skills in C++, Rust, or Go.
* Experience designing and operating distributed systems at scale.
* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent practical experience.

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

* Master's or PhD in Computer Science.
* Experience with Linux internals, container runtimes, or kernel programming.
* Track record of delivering complex projects with broad organizational impact.
* Experience with AWS or similar cloud platforms.

COMPENSATION

Base pay range: USD $185,300 - USD $360,300 / year. Compensation also includes equity
and sign-on bonus.
